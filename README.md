🚗 Austo Analytics Project
📌 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer data from an automobile company (Austo Motor Company) to improve the effectiveness of marketing campaigns using data-driven insights. The analysis helps identify customer behavior patterns and supports strategic decision-making.

🎯 Objective
Analyze customer dataset to understand purchasing behavior
Identify key factors influencing vehicle selection
Validate business assumptions using data
Provide actionable insights for targeted marketing
📊 Dataset Information
Total Records: 1581
Total Features: 14
Data Types: Integer, Float, Object
Includes:
Demographics (Age, Gender, Marital Status)
Financial Data (Salary, Partner Salary, Total Salary)
Customer Behavior (Loans, Dependents, Profession)
Product Info (Vehicle Type: SUV, Sedan, Hatchback)
🛠️ Work Performed
🔹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
Handled missing values in Gender and Partner Salary
Fixed inconsistent entries (e.g., “Femle”, “Female”)
Ensured data consistency for accurate analysis
🔹 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Performed univariate analysis using bar charts and histograms
Conducted bivariate analysis to study relationships between variables
Used visualizations to uncover hidden patterns
🔹 Key Analysis Areas
Customer distribution by gender, profession, and education
Income analysis and purchasing power
Vehicle preference patterns (SUV, Sedan, Hatchback)
Impact of loans and partner income on purchase decisions
📈 Key Insights
Sedans are the most preferred vehicle category
Salaried individuals form the largest customer segment
Young adults (20–30 age group) dominate purchases
Female customers show higher preference for SUVs compared to males
Customers with a working partner tend to purchase higher-priced vehicles
🧠 Business Recommendations
Target salaried professionals for Sedan marketing campaigns
Design SUV campaigns focused on female customers
Offer family-oriented promotions for married customers
Focus on mid-income group (₹50k–₹100k) for maximum conversions
Use segmented marketing strategies based on customer profiles
